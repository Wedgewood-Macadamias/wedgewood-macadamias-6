About Us

Our History

We first started processing macadamias in 2015 as a vertically integrated division supplying kernel to our confectionery business, Wedgewood Nougat. (www.wedgewoodnougat.co.za)

From our humble beginnings of a 1 ton delivery of nut in shell, we slowly grew in experience as the macadamia industry grew in size and matured as a global tree nut crop. Because our primary business was making confectionery, we soon started to specialize in the “Confectionery Grade” market, leaving the bigger “A-Grade” market to the large processors. Fast forward and we are now a stand-alone business passionate about innovating with this magnificent macadamia nut!

Our Vision

We aim to be the most innovative company in the macadamia industry, optimizing value for growers and customers while always putting our planet's needs above profit.

Giving back gives us a real kick and is the major reason why we are still so passionate about the business. We are 100% plastic neutral and working hard towards giving back 1% of turnover to causes that make our world a better place.
