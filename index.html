"The Healthiest Nut"

Macadamias are often hailed as "the healthiest nut", thanks to their exeptional nutritional profile. Their unique combination of nutrients gives them a reputation as a powerhouse for overall wellness.

Macadamias are well known for:

  • Balanced ratio of omega-2 and omega-6
  • Rich in antioxidants
  • High in monounsaturated fat
  • Great source of fibre
  • Packed full of essential nutrients
